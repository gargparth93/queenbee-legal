QueenBee — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 7 July 2026 Last updated: 7 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how QueenBee ("QueenBee", "we", "us", "our") handles information when you use the QueenBee iOS app (the "App"). QueenBee is operated by Parth Garg, based in India (Gurgaon, India). At launch the App is offered to users in the United States, Canada, and India (and is not currently offered in the United Kingdom or European Union). The data-protection law of your country may apply to you (see "Your rights" and "International transfers" below).

We built QueenBee to be private by design. You do not create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email, or contacts.

The short version

  • Your WhatsApp chat files are read on your device. We never receive the original file.
  • To generate a verdict, the App sends the cleaned text of the messages (who said what) to our processing server and on to OpenAI, which produces the analysis. This text is processed to create your result and is not stored by us afterwards.
  • Your past verdicts are saved only on your device (the last 10), not on our servers.
  • Payments and subscriptions are handled by Apple and RevenueCat. We never see your card details.
  • We use Mixpanel to collect anonymous usage analytics — which screens and actions you use — to improve the App. This never includes your chat content or verdicts.
  • We do not show ads, and we do not sell your data.

1. Information we process

a) Chat content you choose to analyse. When you add a WhatsApp chat export (`.txt` or `.zip`), the App opens and parses it locally on your device. Before any analysis, the App extracts the message text and sender labels (up to the most recent 1,000 messages per chat) and transmits that cleaned text to our backend, which forwards it to OpenAI to generate the verdict. The original export file is never uploaded. The transmitted text is used only to produce your verdict and is not retained on our servers after the response is returned.

b) Verdict results. Your generated verdicts (names as they appear in the chat, the metric outcomes, and the summary lines) are stored locally on your device using the operating system's standard local storage. We keep only your most recent 10 verdicts there. They are not transmitted to or stored by us. Deleting the App removes them.

c) Subscription information. QueenBee offers an auto-renewable subscription purchased through Apple's In-App Purchase. Billing is handled entirely by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage and verify subscription status. Through RevenueCat we receive your subscription status and an anonymous subscriber identifier; we do not receive your Apple ID, name, email, or payment details.

d) A device/usage identifier for fair use. To enforce a fair-use limit (approximately 100 comparisons per week) and prevent abuse of our service, we associate each comparison with an anonymous identifier — either the RevenueCat subscriber identifier or a random per-install identifier generated on your device. This identifier is not linked to your real-world identity.

f) Product-analytics data. To understand how the App is used and improve it, we use Mixpanel to collect anonymous usage and product-interaction events — for example, opening the App, requesting a verdict, viewing the paywall, completing or restoring a purchase, and sharing a card. This is behavioural metadata only: it never includes your chat content, the names in your chats, or your verdict text. These events are associated with the same kind of anonymous identifier described above, not with your real-world identity.

e) Messages of other people in your chats. A one-on-one chat necessarily contains the messages of the other person as well as your own. When you choose to analyse a chat, that other person's messages are handled in exactly the same way as yours — parsed on your device, with only the cleaned text sent for analysis and not retained by us afterwards. You decide whether to upload a chat, and you are responsible for ensuring you have the right and any necessary consent to share the other person's messages with us. We act only on your instruction; we do not independently collect, seek out, or store anyone's messages. Deleting a verdict or uninstalling the App removes the related content from your device.

Please do not upload sensitive information. QueenBee is meant for casual, everyday chats. Please do not upload conversations that contain sensitive or special-category information — such as health, sexual life or orientation, religious or political beliefs, racial or ethnic origin, or financial-account details — about you or anyone else, or any chat involving a child. We do not ask for this information and do not want it; if you choose to upload a chat that contains it, that decision and its consequences are yours.

We do NOT collect: your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, or advertising identifiers. The only analytics we collect is the anonymous, behavioural product-interaction data described in (f) — never your chat content.

2. How we use information

We process the information above to:

  • parse your chosen chats and generate the comparison verdict you requested;
  • operate the App's core features and your local verdict history;
  • verify your subscription and provide access to paid features;
  • enforce the weekly fair-use limit and protect against abuse and fraud;
  • understand how the App is used and improve its features (anonymous product analytics);
  • comply with legal obligations.

Legal bases (UK/EU GDPR), where applicable: performance of our contract with you (providing the verdict and subscription you request) and our legitimate interests (operating the service securely and preventing abuse).

4. Data retention

  • Chat text sent for analysis: processed transiently to generate your verdict and not stored by us afterwards. Our providers may retain transmitted content briefly for security and abuse-prevention purposes under their own policies.
  • Verdict history: stored on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
  • Fair-use records: the anonymous usage identifier and comparison timestamps are kept on a rolling basis (about 7 days) to enforce the weekly limit.

5. Security

We use encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data in transit and rely on reputable infrastructure providers. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to or restrict certain processing. These may arise under the CCPA/CPRA and other state laws (United States, including California), PIPEDA (Canada), or the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), among others.

  • Because most data stays on your device, you can delete individual verdicts in the App at any time (tap the ✕ on any entry in your Hive), or remove your entire history by uninstalling the App.
  • We hold no persistent server-side store of your chats. For questions about subscription data held via RevenueCat, or to exercise any right, contact us at the address below.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your Apple ID settings (Settings → your name → Subscriptions).
  • We do not "sell" or "share" your personal information as those terms are used under California and other U.S. state privacy laws, and we do not use it for targeted advertising.

7. Children

QueenBee is not directed to children. You must be at least 17 to use the App. We do not knowingly process information from children below that age.

8. International transfers

We operate the App from India, and our providers (including Supabase and OpenAI) may process the cleaned chat text on servers located in the United States or other countries. This means that when you use the App, your information may be transferred to and processed in India, the United States, and elsewhere — including outside your home country. Where required, such transfers are made under appropriate safeguards.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide notice in the App.
